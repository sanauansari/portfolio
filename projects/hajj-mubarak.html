Hajj Mubarak — Animated Greeting Card

After Effects Illustration Animation

An original animated digital greeting card celebrating Hajj, designed entirely in After Effects for GDS 081, Motion Graphics. Every element — typography, mosque silhouettes, the crescent moon, and decorative motifs — was created from scratch, without any photos or found graphics.

Role

Motion Designer & Illustrator — concept, illustration, and animation.

Tools

Adobe After Effects

About the project

This project asked students to design a digital animated greeting card for an occasion of their choice — entirely original, with no use of outside photos, videos, or found graphics. I chose Hajj as a personal and meaningful occasion to design around.

The card layers foreground, midground, and background elements to build depth, using animated typography and original illustrated graphics including a mosque silhouette and crescent moon. At least one "Add" transform technique covered in class — such as Trim Paths, Repeater, or Wiggler — was used to bring the piece to life within a 5–25 second runtime.
