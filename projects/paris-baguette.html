Paris Baguette — Animated Logo Concepts

After Effects Brand Design Animation

Three distinct animated logo concepts for Paris Baguette Bakery Café, created for GDS 081, Motion Graphics. Each animation explores a different motion approach, using brand-aligned imagery — steaming coffee, falling coffee beans, and café pastries — to communicate warmth and freshness.

Role

Motion Designer — concept development and animation for all three logo treatments.

Tools

Adobe After Effects

About the project

This project asked students to create three distinct animated logo concepts for an existing company — original animation ideas applied to an existing brand, exploring how motion design communicates identity, message, and personality. I chose Paris Baguette as a brand built around warmth, freshness, and café culture.

Each animation explores a different motion approach: one features the logo rising in the steam of a coffee cup, another shows the logo forming as coffee beans fall and settle, and the third incorporates café pastries to round out the brand's sensory identity.
