Reduce Food Waste App

UX Design Mobile Smartwatch Figma

A two-phase UX project designing an app that helps people track and use food before it expires. Phase 1 (GDS 070) covered the full mobile experience — virtual fridge, pantry tracking, recipe suggestions, and QR scanning. Phase 2 (GDS 077) extended the design to Apple Watch, with expiration reminders, cooking timers, and chef coaching sessions.

Role

UX Designer — mobile app design (Phase 1) and smartwatch companion design (Phase 2).

Tools

Figma

The problem

Food often gets forgotten in pantries and fridges, leading to it being thrown away before it's even used. People forget about it, aren't sure if it's still safe to eat, or don't know how to use up leftovers or odd ingredients after a long day. This results in unnecessary food waste.

The solution

The Reduce Food Waste app helps users track and use food in their homes before it expires. It suggests recipes based on available ingredients, tracks expiration dates with reminders, and provides a freshness guide for each item stored in the virtual pantry and fridge. The goal is to reduce food waste one home at a time, save money, and encourage smarter food choices.

In Phase 1, I designed the mobile app. For Phase 2, I designed the smartwatch version to accompany it, offering quick access to expiration reminders, recipe suggestions, key features on the go, notifications, and voice commands.

Target users

  • College students & young professionals — don't have time to track expiration dates manually but want quick recipe suggestions.
  • Environmentally conscious consumers — mindful of food waste and sustainability.
  • Parents — want an easy way to involve kids in food prep and teach them to reduce waste.
  • Roommates — share groceries and need a collaborative way to track and use up shared food.

Key features

  • Voice command integration
  • User-defined push notifications
  • Quick-glance view of fridge & pantry items
  • Expiration date reminders
  • Shopping list sync
  • Voice-assisted cooking mode
