The Secret of the Old Clock — Book Cover Redesign

Print Typography Book Cover

A full book jacket redesign — front cover, back cover, and spine — for the classic Nancy Drew mystery by Carolyn Keene, created for GDS 064, Introduction to Adobe Photoshop. A teal and gold palette with vintage-inspired typography and illustrated mystery motifs brings a fresh, modern feel to the cover while staying true to the story's spirit.

Role

Graphic Designer — full book jacket redesign including front cover, back cover, and spine.

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Overview

This redesign reimagines the classic Nancy Drew mystery with a teal and gold palette and vintage-inspired typography. Mystery motifs — a flashlight, magnifying glass, and antique clock charm — were layered throughout the cover to build atmosphere while keeping the design clean and readable. A free book mockup was used to present the spine, back cover, and front cover together.
